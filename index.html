How to Use This Portal

Reading Posts

Tap any row to open the full post. Read on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. A floating bar at the bottom of every post lets you approve or flag as you go.

Live Sync

Every decision you make syncs instantly to MDM in real time. The green dot in the top-right confirms your connection. No need to export — MDM sees your progress live.

Email Alerts

MDM receives an email at info@thecreatorie.com each time you approve, flag, or reject a post. Your note (if any) is included in the notification.
